The BMI (Body Mass Index) Calculator is a simple tool that helps individuals determine their BMI based on their weight and height. BMI is a numerical value derived from a person's weight and height, used as a screening indicator to categorize individuals into different weight categories such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obese.

Features
Input Fields:
Weight: Enter your weight in kilograms or pounds.
Height: Enter your height in centimeters, meters, or feet and inches.
Calculation:

The calculator uses the following formula for metric units:

BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)2

or for imperial units:

BMI = (weight (lb) × 703) / height (in) 2

Results:

BMI Value: Displays the calculated BMI value.

Weight Category: Categorizes the BMI value into one of the following ranges:
Underweight: BMI < 18.5
Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
Obesity: BMI ≥ 30
